# EXOGRAMMAR — the design law for every CODEX surface

*v9.3 doctrine (2026-06-11). The user's charge: "this is an intergalactic
spiritual learning interface… when your creators evolve this model, this
interface should not even exist but the code running forever… this should
be usable even for an extraterrestrial."*

Everyone brushes teeth with a toothbrush and nobody asks why. There are
infinite ways; the world settled. We are not settled. Every panel must
re-earn its **form**, not just its existence.

## The seven laws

1. **The toothbrush test.** For each panel ask: *if no one had ever built
   a Bible-study tool, would this shape emerge from the data itself?*
   A commentary "panel of paragraphs" is a book habit. A cross-reference
   "list" is a concordance habit. The data has a shape — find it.

2. **Shape before script.** Strip every word from the panel; a being with
   eyes but no English must still read the structure: what is central,
   what is connected, what is contested, what is moving, what matters
   more. Position, brightness, size, motion, line-weight carry meaning
   FIRST; words annotate, never carry alone. (This is the extraterrestrial
   clause — and it's also just what makes Apple Apple.)

3. **Instruments, not documents.** If a panel is a scroll of prose with a
   header, it is antiquated by definition. Show the structure; prose is
   the zoom-in you earn by touching the structure. The Galaxy is the
   canonical proof: 415K cross-references became a sky.

4. **One gesture deep.** The thing itself is the control. Click the star,
   pull the thread, drag the era. No "open settings to change view".
   Anything two menus away may as well not exist.

5. **The interface dies; the code lives forever.** Panels are mortal skins.
   Engines are immortal: every capability MUST exist as a kernel tool
   (`CODEX_KERNEL.call`) or pure data module callable without any DOM.
   When a future model replaces this interface overnight, it must lose
   nothing but pixels. No logic in JSX that isn't a thin projection of an
   engine.

6. **Honesty is load-bearing.** CONTESTED stamps, SCHOLARLY SURVEY · NOT
   PREDICTION banners, source cites — these survive every redesign.
   Beauty that hides uncertainty is propaganda.

7. **Scripture is sacred ground.** Serif, serene, honey-flowing, free of
   chrome. Instruments speak mono at the edges. The Word is never made to
   compete with its own commentary.

## The verdict scale

- **STAR** — already an instrument; form earned (Galaxy, Sword cleave).
- **MOLT** — right idea, antiquated skin; reshape in place.
- **REMAKE** — document pretending to be an instrument; rebuild from the
  data's own shape.
- **FOLD** — capability belongs inside another instrument; panel dies,
  engine lives.

Panel-by-panel verdicts live in the work queue (memory + tasks), not
here — this file is law, not ledger.
